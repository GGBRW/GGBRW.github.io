A digital logic simulator

BOOLR is a digital logic simulator. We are three Dutch high school students and we made BOOLR for a school project. BOOLR was created with love and JavaScript.
We used Electron to convert it to a desktop application. We have built this application to make an 8-bits computer called Tineke, you can download her below.
